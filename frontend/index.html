The process

Three inputs. One number you can act on.

We built this for the person who's tired of calling three agents and getting three wildly different answers.

01

Pick the neighbourhood

Choose from 172 Bengaluru localities — from Koramangala to Devanahalli. Our model treats each area separately, because ₹5,000/sqft means very different things in different parts of the city.

02

Tell us about the property

Total square footage, number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms. That's it. Our model was trained on these exact inputs from 13,320 actual sales — so it knows what matters and what's noise.

03

Get a real estimate

In under a second you'll see a price in lakhs — the same format agents use, but without the markup. Use it to negotiate, to verify a listing price, or just to understand the market before you commit.
